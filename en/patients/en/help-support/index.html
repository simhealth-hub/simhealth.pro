Help & Support

If you feel at risk, overwhelmed, or unsure what to do next, you deserve immediate support. This page shares trusted crisis resources you can use right now.

View resources Contact SimHealth

Important

SimHealth is currently in development and does not provide emergency care, crisis counselling, or medical advice. If you are in immediate danger, call your local emergency number right now.
