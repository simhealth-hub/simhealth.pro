How SimHealth is intended to help

SimHealth is being developed as a clinician-informed digital companion designed to support people during high-risk moments (for example, urges, stress spikes, or late-night “just one bet” thoughts).

  • Safety-first: clear signposting to real support services and no emergency claims.
  • No medical claims: outcomes will only be discussed publicly when properly evaluated.
  • Privacy-minded: any future pilot data collection would be consent-led and minimised.
  • Clinical governance: intended for supervised pilots before any broader use.

Important: SimHealth is not a substitute for professional care. If you feel unsafe or at risk, please use Crisis Resources.
