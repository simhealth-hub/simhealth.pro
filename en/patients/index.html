For Patients

A clinical digital companion designed to help manage gambling urges safely, without real-world financial harm.

What SimHealth Will Offer

SimHealth is being developed as a supportive tool alongside existing treatment. It is not a gambling product, and it does not use real money.

  • Simulated betting and games using fictive currency
  • Clinical cues and reflection during emotional highs and lows
  • Designed to reduce relapse and drop-out rates
  • Built in collaboration with healthcare professionals

Availability

SimHealth is currently in development and not publicly available. Access will initially be provided through clinical partners.
