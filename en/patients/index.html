For Patients

A safer way to ride out gambling urges

SimHealth is being designed as a clinical companion to help you pause, reset, and make safer choices when cravings hit. This is a preview site — features are coming step by step.

What SimHealth will help with

  • Interrupting the urge loop Short tools designed for the moment cravings spike — before decisions turn into damage.
  • Practicing control safely Simulated experiences can help you face triggers without real-world financial harm.
  • Support that respects privacy Clear, calm guidance — with an emphasis on safety and responsibility.

What SimHealth is not

  • No real-money gambling SimHealth is not a betting product, casino, or sportsbook.
  • Not a replacement for professional care It’s a support tool that can complement therapy and recovery plans.

In development: patient accounts and structured modules will be introduced during pilot phases. For now, this site shares the direction and safety approach.
