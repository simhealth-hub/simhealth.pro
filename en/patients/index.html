What SimHealth is

SimHealth is a clinician-led project being developed to support people who experience gambling urges in real-world situations. The aim is not to promote gambling, but to help regulate the emotional and cognitive response that can lead to loss of control.

Why this approach exists

Many relapses happen outside clinical settings — late at night, after stress, during social triggers, or when emotions feel too heavy. SimHealth is designed around the moments when urges actually occur, with clinical governance and safeguards guiding development from day one.

Current status

SimHealth is not yet publicly available. The system is being prepared for supervised clinical pilots with hospital partners. Until then, this site provides a high-level overview and directs visitors to appropriate support resources.
