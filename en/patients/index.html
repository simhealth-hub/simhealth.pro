For Patients

A calmer, more supported way to face gambling urges — without judgement and without real-world risk.

What SimHealth is

SimHealth is a clinically informed digital companion being developed to support people who are trying to manage gambling-related urges. It is designed to complement existing recovery strategies and, where appropriate, professional care.

The focus of SimHealth is on providing structured, supportive guidance during moments when urges feel strongest — helping create space between the urge and the action.

How SimHealth may help

  • Offer support during moments of heightened urge or emotional pressure
  • Help create a pause before decisions are made
  • Increase awareness of personal triggers and patterns
  • Encourage safer responses in a controlled, supportive environment
  • Support continuity between clinical sessions or recovery milestones

What SimHealth is not

SimHealth is not a gambling platform and does not involve real money. It is not a replacement for therapy, medical treatment, or professional support.

It is intended to be used responsibly and, where appropriate, alongside guidance from qualified healthcare professionals.

Current status

SimHealth is currently under development. Initial access will be limited to pilot settings and clinical collaborations, with patient safety and ethical use guiding each stage of release.
