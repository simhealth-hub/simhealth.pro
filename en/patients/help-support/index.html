Help & Support

If you’re struggling with gambling urges — or you feel at risk right now — you deserve support immediately. Below are trusted, real-world helplines and services by country.

Country
Gambling support If you feel unsafe
If you feel in immediate danger:

Call your local emergency number now (for example, 112 in much of Europe, 999 in the UK, 911 in the US). This SimHealth website does not provide emergency care — the services below can.

Gambling support (by country)

These services are independent from SimHealth. They are included because patient safety and clinician responsibility come first. If you’re a clinician reviewing this page, this is intentionally conservative: direct pathways to regulated support (not app-based promises).

United Kingdom

Confidential gambling support, information, and referral.

Support for problem gambling and addiction guidance.

  • Joueurs Info Service: 09 74 75 13 13
  • Website: joueurs-info-service.fr

United States

National problem gambling support and crisis options.

  • National Problem Gambling Helpline (NCPG): 1-800-MY-RESET 4
  • Text: text “RESET” to 800 5
  • If you are in emotional crisis: call or text 988 (Suicide & Crisis Lifeline) 6
Call 1-800-MY-RESET Text RESET to 800 Call 988
