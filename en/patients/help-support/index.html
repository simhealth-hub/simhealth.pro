Quick guidance

SimHealth is a professional project showcase. These contacts are provided because safety matters more than anything. If you feel at risk of harming yourself, call your local emergency number or a crisis line now.

Important: SimHealth is in development and does not provide emergency services. This page links you to real support organisations in each country.

Crisis & suicide support

Tap a number to call. On phones, it should open your dialer automatically.

France

National suicide prevention line (free). If there’s immediate danger, call emergency services.

☎ 3114 ☎ 112 (Emergency)
Official: 3114

United States

988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline (call or text). For immediate danger, call 911.

☎ 988 Text 988 ☎ 911 (Emergency)
Official: 988 Lifeline

Canada

988 Suicide Crisis Helpline (call or text). For immediate danger, call 911.

☎ 988 Text 988 ☎ 911 (Emergency)
Official: Canada 988

United Kingdom & ROI

Samaritans (freephone). For emergency services: 999 (UK) / 112.

☎ 116 123 ☎ 999 (Emergency) ☎ 112 (Emergency)
Official: Samaritans

Australia

Lifeline crisis support. If there’s immediate danger: 000.

☎ 13 11 14 ☎ 000 (Emergency)
Official: Lifeline

New Zealand

Call or text 1737 for free support from a trained counsellor.

☎ 1737 Text 1737 ☎ 111 (Emergency)
Official: 1737
