Help & Support

If you are struggling with gambling urges, anxiety, or feel at risk, confidential support is available. This page lists trusted services by country.

Immediate support (if you feel unsafe)

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number right now. If you can, also contact someone you trust and avoid being alone.
