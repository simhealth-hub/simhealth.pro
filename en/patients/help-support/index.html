Help & Support

If you are struggling with gambling urges, anxiety, or feel at risk, confidential support is available. This page lists trusted services by country.

Immediate support (if you feel unsafe)

If you or someone else is in immediate danger, call your local emergency number right now. If you can, also contact someone you trust and avoid being alone.

Clinical note: SimHealth is a research project in development. The resources below are independent services that can help you today.

1) Pause the moment

Put distance between you and betting apps/sites. Close tabs. Step into a different room.

2) Call or text support

Use your country’s helpline below. You don’t need to “be sure” — just reach out.

3) Reduce access

If possible, ask someone trusted to help with finances and install gambling blockers.

Find support by country

We start with the most common English-speaking regions plus France (SimHealth’s home base), then list additional options below.

United Kingdom

Confidential gambling support and advice (24/7 availability varies by service).

GamCare Support & chat GAMSTOP Self-exclusion NHS Guidance

If you’re in crisis or feel unsafe, use emergency services (999) or NHS urgent support pathways.

United States

National crisis support plus problem gambling resources.

988 Lifeline Call/Text/Chat NCPG Help & treatment SAMHSA Treatment locator

In emergency: call 911. For mental health crisis support: 988.

Australia

National crisis line and gambling support.

Lifeline 24/7 crisis Gambling Help Online Chat & support

In emergency: call 000. Lifeline (13 11 14) is widely used for crisis support.

Canada

Support varies by province — start with national crisis support and provincial services.

Crisis Services Canada National ConnexOntario Ontario

For immediate danger call 911. For province-specific gambling resources, use local health portals.

France

National support resources and guidance for addictive behaviours.

Joueurs Info Service Support Drogues Info Service Guidance

In emergency: call 112 (EU emergency) or 15 (SAMU).

Ireland

National crisis support and local addiction services.

Samaritans Ireland Support HSE Mental health

In emergency: call 112 or 999.

Other countries & Europe

Europe (general)

Emergency number works across the EU/EEA. For country-specific lines, use national health services.

EU emergency info 112

EU emergency: 112. If you are in immediate danger, call it now.

New Zealand

National crisis support and gambling harm services.

1737 Call/Text Gambling Helpline NZ Support

International directory

If your country isn’t listed, these directories can help you find local support quickly.

Find A Helpline Directory Gamblers Anonymous Meetings

If you share your country with your clinician, they can help identify regulated local services.
