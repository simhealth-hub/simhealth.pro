Supervised Digital Support

Pre-Pilot Explainer

Read overview Contact

What this is

SimHealth is a clinician-informed pre-pilot project exploring whether supervised digital support can complement existing care pathways, particularly during the between-session gap when clinical support is unavailable.

The project is governance-first and safety-first. It makes no therapeutic claims and is not intended for unsupervised or consumer use.

The problem it addresses

Many forms of behavioural and addiction-related harm occur between appointments, often during high-risk emotional states.

In gambling-related harm, this gap is pronounced. SimHealth explores whether governed digital support can help address that gap without bypassing clinical oversight.

Purpose of the pre-pilot

The pre-pilot examines feasibility, governance, workflow integration, and risk boundaries. It does not test outcomes at this stage.

What SimHealth is not

  • Not an emergency or crisis service
  • Not a replacement for therapy
  • Not presented as a medical device
  • Not an unsupervised consumer app

Governance and next steps

No patient-facing deployment occurs without ethics review, institutional approval, and regulatory consideration where applicable.

Expressing interest opens a professional conversation only.
