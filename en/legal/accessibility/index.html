Accessibility

We’re building SimHealth with accessibility in mind — including clear navigation, readable contrast, and keyboard-friendly controls.

Read statement Report an issue

Last updated: 2026-02-01

Commitment: SimHealth aims to be usable by as many people as possible, including users with visual, motor, cognitive, and attention differences. If you experience any accessibility barriers, tell us and we’ll prioritise fixes.

What we’re doing

  • Readable typography: clear font choices and comfortable line spacing.
  • Contrast-aware UI: dark/light modes to support different preferences.
  • Keyboard navigation: interactive controls usable without a mouse.
  • Semantic HTML: structured headings, labels, and landmarks.
  • Mobile-first considerations: layouts adapt for smaller screens.

Known limitations

SimHealth is still in development, so some pages may evolve quickly. If you notice issues (e.g. focus traps, missing labels, low contrast), please report them.

Contact for accessibility issues

Email contact@simhealth.pro with a short description and (if possible) your device and browser.
