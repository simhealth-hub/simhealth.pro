Cookies

A clear summary of cookies and local storage used by simhealth.pro.

Read cookie notice Privacy

Effective date: 2026-02-01

Summary: SimHealth aims to minimise tracking. The current website primarily uses storage only to remember user preferences (e.g., theme). If analytics or additional cookies are added later, this notice will be updated and (where required) consent mechanisms will be implemented.

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. Similar technologies include local storage and session storage. Some are necessary for basic functionality; others may be used for measurement/analytics.

2) What we currently use

At this time, simhealth.pro is designed to work without advertising trackers. The main “preference” we store is your theme choice (dark/light).

  • Theme preference: stored to remember your selection between visits (e.g., via localStorage).

3) What we do not do

  • No ad tracking: we do not run behavioural advertising trackers on purpose.
  • No sale of personal data: we do not sell browsing data.

4) Managing cookies / storage

You can remove stored preferences by clearing your browser cookies/site data for simhealth.pro. You can also browse in private/incognito mode.

5) Future analytics

If privacy-preserving analytics are introduced, we will update this page and implement appropriate consent controls where required by law.
