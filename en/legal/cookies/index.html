Cookies

A clear summary of cookies and local storage used by simhealth.pro.

Read cookie notice Privacy

Effective date: 2026-02-01

Summary: SimHealth aims to minimise tracking. The current website primarily stores preferences only (for example, theme selection). If analytics or additional cookies are introduced, this page will be updated and consent mechanisms added where required.

1) What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device by websites. Similar technologies include localStorage and session storage. Some are essential for basic functionality; others may be used for measurement or analytics.

2) What we currently use

At present, simhealth.pro is designed to operate without advertising trackers. The main preference stored is your theme choice (dark or light).

  • Theme preference: stored to remember your selection between visits (e.g. via localStorage).

3) What we do not do

  • No advertising trackers: we do not intentionally deploy behavioural advertising trackers.
  • No data sales: we do not sell browsing or personal data.

4) Managing cookies and storage

You can remove stored preferences by clearing site data for simhealth.pro in your browser. You can also browse in private or incognito mode.

5) Future analytics

If privacy-respecting analytics are introduced, this notice will be updated and appropriate consent controls implemented in line with applicable regulations.
