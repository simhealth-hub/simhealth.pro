Regulatory Status & Governance

Project status

SimHealth is an early-stage digital health research project in pre-pilot development. It is not a medical device, clinical service, or emergency intervention.

Intended purpose

SimHealth explores whether clinician-guided, safety-first digital tools could support people experiencing gambling-related harm between clinical contacts, under professional oversight.

What SimHealth is not

  • Not medical advice or diagnosis
  • Not emergency or crisis care
  • Not automated clinical decision-making
  • Not a replacement for professional judgment

Regulatory positioning

SimHealth is not currently classified as a medical device. If future development introduces regulated functionality, appropriate regulatory pathways will be assessed before deployment.

Clinical governance

Any future patient-facing use would occur only within supervised, ethically approved contexts with appropriate clinical oversight.

Research & ethics

Professional feedback activities are consent-led, non-clinical, and do not involve patient-identifying information.

Data protection & privacy

Data handling follows GDPR principles, privacy-by-design, and data minimisation practices.

Jurisdiction

SimHealth is currently anchored in the European Union (France).

Transparency

This page will be updated as the project evolves. No claims of clinical effectiveness are made at this stage.
