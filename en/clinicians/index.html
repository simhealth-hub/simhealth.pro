Clinical governance priorities

SimHealth is being built with a safety-first approach suitable for clinical settings. Pilot access is intended to be supervised and documented, with clear boundaries and responsible communication.

  • No emergency claims: clear signposting to real crisis services.
  • Evidence discipline: no public outcomes without appropriate evaluation.
  • Privacy-first: consent-led, minimised data; governance defined with partners.
  • Ethics readiness: protocol clarity, documentation, and oversight when required.
