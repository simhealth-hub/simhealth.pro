Under the hood (pre-pilot concept)

SimHealth is being designed as a supervised two-sided programme: a patient-facing experience to support structured, time-limited urge-management sessions, and a clinician portal to support governance, oversight, and evaluation planning.

  • Patient layer: structured sessions, reflection prompts, safer exits, signposting, no real-money gambling.
  • Clinician layer: oversight-focused views and feasibility metrics (designed with services, not direct-to-consumer).
  • Governance layer: consent-first, data minimisation, deletion pathway, DPIA-led planning with partners.
