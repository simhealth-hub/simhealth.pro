About SimHealth (summary)

SimHealth is a clinician-informed digital health project exploring how supervised, safety-first digital tools might support patients between sessions, particularly during high-risk or vulnerable moments. The concept uses a dual-facing model: a clinician-side environment designed for governance and oversight, and a patient-side experience intended to deliver time-limited, clinician-defined digital support aligned with therapeutic goals.

Boundaries: SimHealth is currently in pre-pilot development. It is not emergency care, not a medical device, and does not replace clinical judgment. This questionnaire is for professional feedback only.

Important: Please do not include patient-identifying information or clinical case details. If you need to discuss anything sensitive, use the Contact page instead.
